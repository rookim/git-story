A young man's family is brutally slaughtered while he was out working, and the only survivor-his sister-has turned into a demon. This young man Tanjiro embarks on a journey to seek out the demon that killed their family and turn his sister Nezuko back into a human. Tanjiro becomes a demon slayer towards this cause.
